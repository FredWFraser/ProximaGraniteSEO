Etymology of Our Name

"Proxima" generally refers to Proxima Centauri, the closest star to our Sun. It is a red dwarf star located in the constellation Centaurus, approximately 4.24 light-years away. "Granite" refers to New Hampshire being known as the "Granite State". Granite also symbolizes strength, durability and endurance. Just like that star, we strive to be the clear choice for New Hampshire businesses searching online.
