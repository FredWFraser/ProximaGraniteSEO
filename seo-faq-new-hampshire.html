What is SEO in your own words?

SEO stands for "Search Engine Optimization". It is the art and science of taking an existing website and making it searchable in Google and other search engines including AI. It is the process of doing this organically meaning not through the use of paid ads.



Why do I need SEO?

An SEO optimized website helps your business get more visibility on the web (also called "impressions") which leads to more clicks. More clicks and time spent on your website leads to more business.



How long does it take to see results from SEO?

An established site can take 1-3 months to see results. This is a crucial period for website audits, keyword research, and technical SEO optimizations. A new site can take up to 6 months.
