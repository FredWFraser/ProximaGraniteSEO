The New Age of Search By Fred Fraser



How Google's AI Agent Will Change Search and SEO in 2026 and Beyond



There is a new kid in town. Let me introduce to you “Google-Agent”.


Google has introduced a new user agent, named “Google-Agent”. It shows up when AI visits your site. AI is acting on behalf of a user. Just like a user, this secret agent (just kidding) can do things like browse pages, compare information, fill out forms. Perhaps someday complete transactions for you.


Compare Google-Agent to Googlebot. Googlebot is an old, familiar bot that we know and love, that serves the purpose of visiting your pages, crawling (accessing the links of your pages and going to the next one). Hopefully, you've done your SEO and linked your pages to other pages on your site. Google-Agent acts like a user with intent.


This is such a huge deal in SEO because SEO is no longer about ranking for clicks. It's about the shift to something called agentic web. This is about your website being usable by literal machines that behave just like users.


Very science fiction right?


Well, we are living in the science fiction age for SEO. We are able to setup tasks in business using AI agents for our workflows. We do AI-augmented coding in our software development work. There was always talk about moving towards a cashless society. Picture a browser-less, click-less world. Today, we have a “Siri”. We call out to Siri to do something for us. We will call out to an AI agent “find me the best plumber in Plaistow, New Hampshire”. This agent will surf the web as we humans do now, and based on certain cues and signals will make the recommendations to us. Perhaps this agent will go on to even setup the appointment for us. Again, no browser, no clicks. No thought at all, the task is completed.


Scary? Yes but a very real possibility and we are almost there.


What does this mean for SEO today? The old world of SEO is one we are all familiar with. Ranking in Google, getting clicks, users visit our sites, users make thoughtful, informed decisions based on what they see and think. What is coming is as I mentioned, the user asks AI “find me the best xyz”. The AI agent does the searching, does the evaluation, does the choosing, and acts. Your website may never a get a traditional visit. This is handled by machine.


SEO is used to get impressions, rankings, click through rate. That is SEO of a bygone era. Now, SEO is moving towards, getting selected by the agent, and as important, can the agent perform the task on your site. Now this is getting into sci-fi territory. Someone, or more correctly, some THING will do the thinking and acting on your behalf. Are you ready for it?


SEO will not be about traffic necessarily, but about getting direct conversions from AI. It will not be all about getting traffic and visits to your website. This is the “zero click” web theory.


What does all this mean for SEO? Traditional SEO is not getting replaced. It will matter more than ever. Ranking will still matter. Authority and content will matter. But this is the shift. You will need machine-readable structure, clear answers, quality of content and your workflow must work without any problems. This is where quality assurance testing matters.


This is a brief look into the future of search, starting with Google-Agent. The future of things always seems a bit scary and exciting all at the same time, and the same holds true for the future of SEO. This is an exciting time to be part of SEO, to help shape the future of search and to bravely go where search is heading.
