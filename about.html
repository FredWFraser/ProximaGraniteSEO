Who We Are

I took several courses and eventually was led to a course in full stack web development. It was a learning experience, with lots of highs and some lows and was the toughest course I ever took. I thought this was something I could do at home as a business. I also discovered something called SEO, which I was previously unaware of, and took a couple of courses. I then a took a course on WordPress. I wanted to use all my newly found knowledge and not be stuck forever in a place called "tutorial hell". So here I am embarking on a business adventure to see where it will lead. I have worked recently as a software QA engineer, briefly as a test technician, as a bill collector and office manager. I love to work with others and I come from a technical space so I can work comfortably with a developer if the need arises, or work with you on the technical aspects of your website. I have worked in a collaborive environment and not afraid of opposing viewpoints. I appreciate the diversity of ideas. As my company begins and grows, I am thankful for the chance you would be a part of my journey, as well as me being a part of yours. In my personal time, I enjoy songwriting and producing music in my home studio, as well as distance running.
